Colophon

Kwon.nyc is owned, operated, designed, written, edited, maintained, flavored, and (mostly) built by me, Rachel J. Kwon, with help from the below.

I use Hugo and DeployHQ to generate and deploy the site, respectively, and Github to host the source code (changelog here). Locally, I use Sublime Text to write and edit code, as well as Markdown files which constitute the content. (More gear and tech here.)

This cool illustration of my visage was done by my friend Leigh Peterson, who also taught me how to bathe a cat. (Those were two separate favors.)

I don’t really use design software to design websites, but I probably should. It’s just that every time I try, I find myself gravitating back towards designing in the browser.

Typefaces I’m using are New Spirit for headlines and the Apple system font (currently San Francisco, I guess) for everything else.

A note on privacy and data: I don't use cookies or collect any of your data on this site. I don't sell anything or make any money on this site. I do use a cookie-free analytics platform called Plausible for basic metrics. The metrics I am most interested in are referring pages (to see who cares enough to link to this site and to maybe make new friends üòÄ), and visitor counts in aggregate (to get a sense of how many people are looking at specific things I've written, and, I'll be honest, to get a small but nontoxic ego boost). What I am NOT interested in is tracking what individual visitors are doing.
