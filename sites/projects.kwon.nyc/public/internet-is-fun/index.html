The internet used to be fun

I’ve been meaning to write some kind of Important Thinkpiece™ on the glory days of the early internet, but every time I sat down to do it, I would find others that were already out there. All of these articles answer the question “Why have a personal website?” with “Because it’s fun, and the internet used to be fun” to some degree. Some are the author’s story of why and how they have their own website; others are more pleading, manifesto-flavored calls to action.

If you’ve written something that feels like it belongs here—especially if your voice is one that’s underrepresented—I’d be interested to read it! Holler at me by email (kwon at fastmail.com) or on mastodon.social/@rjkwon.
