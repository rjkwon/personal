Colophon

Kwon.nyc is owned, operated, designed, written, edited, maintained, flavored, and (mostly) built by me, Rachel J. Kwon, with help from the below.

I use Hugo and DeployHQ to generate and deploy the site, respectively, and Bitbucket to host the source code. Locally, I use Byword to write and edit Markdown files, and Sublime Text to write and edit code.

The incredibly detailed illustration of my visage was done by my friend Leigh Peterson, who also taught me how to bathe a cat. (Those were two separate favors.)

I don’t really use design software to design websites, but I probably should. It’s just that every time I try, I find myself gravitating back towards designing in the browser.

Typefaces I’m using are New Spirit for headlines and the Apple system font (currently San Francisco, I guess) for everything else.

A note on privacy and data: I don't use cookies or collect any of your data on this site. I don't sell anything or make any money on this site. I do use a cookie-free analytics platform called Plausible to get basic metrics that I use to make the website better.
